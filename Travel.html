Ontario- R.C Harris Water Treatment Plant

Location: 2701 Queen St E, Scarborough, ON M4E 1H4

R.C Harris Water Treatment plant locats in the easte part of the city near the Lake Ontario.

More

The first time I pass by there while driving, its unique view attract me at the first. I thought it was a church or a museum, but it is actually an abandoned water treatment plants.

It has been a popular public area for people to walk dog and enjoy views. There is a huge, beautiful architecture face towards the lake, unfortunatelly, there was a film taking when I want there so i was not allow to take picture of the building.

Besides the building, I can say it is the best place I have ever been to see the view of the Lake Ontario. I would mark this place as a "must go" place.

Mexico is a magic place for me. I went to Cancun about 4 years ago, I am really into island trip, but my budget is a bit short for Hawaii, I went on Expedia and book this 5 days trip with everything inclusive package.

Everything inclusive means that you only need to pay a certain amount to stay in the hotel, and you can use any service in hotel as you want, for free. I basically stay in the hotel for 5 whole days, because you can eat in those nice restuarants as many time as you want for free, the resort itself have a lot of beached for you to swim, you can even have cocktails as many as you want for free.

Kelowna- Kangaroo Creek Farm

Location: 5932 Old Vernon Rd, Kelowna, BC V1X 7V2

My favorite province must be British Columbia, British Columbia can always suprise me.

More

When I was in high school in British Columbia, I always went to road trips. I must say that drive from Vancouver to Kelowna, you will see many incredible views that you have never imagined.

This kangaroo farm is a must go if you ever go to Kelowna. I went to there at around April, there are more Kangaroos than visitors, and those Kangaroos are very lazy and cute.

They also have other small animals that I really do not know their names. this place is super incredible!
