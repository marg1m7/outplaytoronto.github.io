Webpage process

The main goal of my site is to share my life. The three main navigation is OOTD, Travel and Pet, those are three of my main hobbies. Personally I like simple style and use black and white colors to form the main component.

To make my homepage, I did a slideshow to show assorted content in my homepage. I also did a card element as a profolio to introduce myself in timeline.

For the OOTD page, I basically show all my outfit pictures, and because there are many pictures, so I divide them in sections and did an associative navigation in the site that users can click to view a certain section directly. I also did a transform action that when users hover on a picture, the picture will be larger that users can view more clearly.

Travel page is supposed to be a content-heavy page, but users don't like to view much content directly, so I hide main content in embed form so that when users click "more" they can view specific content.

For the pet page, I think pet page is supposed to be more motional. So I include many video content that users can see how my pets look better. I think it will be more attractive to pet lovers.
